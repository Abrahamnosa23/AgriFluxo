Frequently asked

How much does it cost?

MVP pilots offer discounted per-kg fares and subscription plans for frequent users. Final pricing will be dynamic, based on distance, vehicle type and shared-loading options.

Is AgriFluxo available in my area?

We are launching pilots in selected agricultural regions. Join the waitlist to be notified about pilot availability in your area.

How do you verify drivers?

We perform identity checks, vehicle inspections and onboarding training; premium drivers are rated by farmers after each trip.
